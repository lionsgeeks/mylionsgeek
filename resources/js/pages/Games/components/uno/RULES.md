### Initial Setup
- **Players**: 2-4 players
- **Starting Cards**: Each player receives 7 cards
- **First Card**: First card from deck is placed on discard pile
- **Wild First Card**: If first card is Wild, a random color is chosen
- **Turn Order**: Play starts with first player, proceeds clockwise

### 1. Number Cards (0-9)

- **Quantity**:

  - Two of each "1-9" per color
- **Total**: 76 number cards

## ğŸ¯ Playing Rules

### Basic Rules
1. **Match Color OR Number/Action**: You can play a card if it matches:
   - The **color** of the top card, OR
   - The **number/action** of the top card

2. **Wild Cards**: Can be played anytime (Wild Draw 4 has restrictions)

3. **If You Can't Play**:
   - You **must draw 1 card** from the draw pile
   - If the drawn card is playable, you can play it immediately
   - If not playable, your turn ends

4. **Turn Order**:
   - Play proceeds in the current direction (clockwise or counterclockwise)
   - Reverse card changes direction
   - Skip card skips next player

## âš¡ Action Cards

### Skip Card
- **Effect**: Next player loses their turn
- **Play**: Must match color or be a Skip card
- **Result**: Turn moves to player after the skipped player

### Reverse Card
- **Effect**: Changes play direction
- **2 Players**: Acts like Skip (skips opponent)
- **3+ Players**: Reverses direction, turn stays with current player
- **Play**: Must match color or be a Reverse card

### Draw 2 Card
- **Effect**: Next player draws 2 cards and skips their turn
- **Play**: Must match color or be a Draw 2 card
- **Cannot Stack**: If you receive Draw 2, you must draw and skip (cannot play another Draw 2)

## ğŸŒˆ Wild Cards

### Wild Card
- **Effect**: Choose any color for the next player
- **Play**: Can be played anytime
- **Action**: After playing, you choose the color (Red, Green, Blue, or Yellow)

### Wild Draw 4 Card
- **Effect**: Choose any color, next player draws 4 cards and skips turn
- **Restriction**: Can **only** be played if you **don't have** a card matching the current color
- **Challenge**: Other players can challenge if they think you have a matching color
- **Cannot Stack**: If you receive Draw 4, you must draw and skip (cannot play another Draw 4)

## ğŸ¯ UNO Rule

### When to Call UNO
- You **must call "UNO"** when you have **exactly 1 card** left
- You must call UNO **before** the next player starts their turn

### Penalty
- If you **don't call UNO** and the next player starts their turn:
  - You must **draw 2 penalty cards**
  - Game continues

### How to Call UNO
1. When you have 1 card, click the **"Call UNO!"** button
2. Button appears automatically when you have 1 card
3. You can only call UNO when you have exactly 1 card

## ğŸ”„ Special Situations

### Empty Draw Pile
- **What happens**: If draw pile runs out
- **Solution**: Reshuffle discard pile (except top card) to create new draw pile
- **Top card**: Remains on discard pile

### Cannot Play
- **Situation**: You don't have a playable card
- **Action**: Draw 1 card
- **If playable**: You can play it immediately
- **If not playable**: Your turn ends, next player goes

### Draw 2 / Draw 4 Received
- **Rule**: You **cannot stack** Draw 2 or Draw 4
- **Action**: You must draw the cards and skip your turn
- **Cannot play**: You cannot play another Draw 2/4 to pass it on

### Turn Changes
- **UNO Status**: Cleared when turn changes
- **Drawn Card**: If you drew a card, indicator clears when turn changes
- **Pending Draws**: Must be drawn before playing
